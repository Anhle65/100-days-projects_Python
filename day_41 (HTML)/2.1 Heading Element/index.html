Ingredients

Buy ingredients to make cake

For the dough:

  • milk
  • yeast
  • eggs
  • butter
  • bread flour

For the filling:

  • brown sugar
  • ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix milk and yeast, sugar, eggs
  2. Melt butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10'
  5. Transfer to a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap, leave for 2 hours
  6. After the dough is doubled size, roll and shape before bake
